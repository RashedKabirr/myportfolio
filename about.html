About Me

My name is Ahmad Rashed Kabir. I was born in july 1998 in Afghanistan. I started elementary school in Kabul and after 12 years of school I graduated in 2015. Fast forward to late 2017-2018 I started my first job as an English teacher in a private institute. As an English teacher I was responsible for teaching 'speaking & reading' parts and continued to teach for the next 3 years. But at the same time I was very interested in Software Developement and always fascinated by the new technologies that were coming out everyday so I decided to learn Web Developement in 2021 by myself and the first book I read was 'HTML & CSS for dummies' which really helped understand the basics of programming and did my very first project which was the 'Hello World' program. When I wrote the HTML code and the browser interpreted and there was the result on the screen that was the moment I got very excited and I knew that I wanted to be a software developer. And from that time I kept learning and practitng my skills although there was a lot of problems and struggles on my way (the ongoing problems in my country) which caused me to stop the progress And take a pause but since I came to Switzerland I started to continue my studies and now as I am writing this text about myself I feel camfortable with Html & Css and currently learning JavaScript at freecodecamp.org. My final goal is to become a successful software developer and create effective applications to help make this world a better place. I am willing to continue to learn other programming languages as well. And finally to describe myself in one sentence I would say
"I have no special talents. I am only passionately curious."
